Photo and Camera Data Usage

Collection and Purpose

Our app collects and processes photos for the following essential educational purposes:

  • Profile Pictures: To identify students and staff members
  • Document Scanning: To digitize educational materials and assignments
  • Educational Records: To maintain digital copies of important documents

Data Processing

Photos are processed as follows:

  • Storage: Photos are stored securely in the app's private storage
  • Processing: Photos are processed ephemerally (temporarily) for immediate use
  • Access: Only authorized users can access their own photos and shared educational materials

User Control and Rights

Users have complete control over their photo data:

  • Permission Control: Users can grant or deny camera and photo access
  • Data Deletion: Users can delete their uploaded photos at any time
  • Access Limitation: Photos are only accessed when explicitly initiated by the user

Data Protection

We protect photo data through:

  • Secure Storage: All photos are stored in the app's private, encrypted storage
  • Limited Access: Photos are only accessible to authorized users
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Photos are not shared with third parties
  • No Advertising Use: Photos are never used for advertising or marketing purposes
